2. How we use your data

Solely to operate the App: authenticate you, deliver and sync your study content and progress, run the spaced-repetition engine, manage subscriptions, and provide support. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

3. How your data is shared

We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties for their own purposes. We use a small number of service providers strictly to run the App:

These providers process data only on our behalf to deliver the App's features.

4. Data security

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Access to the backend is restricted and authenticated. We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorised access.

We keep your account data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see our Delete Account page. Purchase records may be retained only as required by tax and accounting law.

The App is intended for users 18 years and older preparing for government recruitment examinations. It is not directed at children.
